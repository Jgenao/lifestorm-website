LIFESTORM REFLECTION TOOL

5 Reflection Questions When Life Changes Unexpectedly

Life transitions can bring uncertainty, difficult decisions, and emotions we did not expect. These questions are designed to help you pause, reflect, and identify your next step forward.

Before Moving Forward, Pause and Reflect

You do not need to have every answer today. Sometimes clarity begins with asking yourself better questions.

  1. What storm am I currently facing?
    Name the transition, challenge, decision, or change in front of you.
  2. What emotions or thoughts keep showing up?
    Notice what this season is bringing to the surface.
  3. What is still within my control?
    Separate what you can influence from what you cannot.
  4. What strengths have helped me through previous storms?
    Remember the resilience you have already built.
  5. What is one meaningful next step I can take?
    Focus on movement, not perfection.
