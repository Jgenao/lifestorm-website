FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Questions You May Have

Starting coaching can bring up a lot of questions—and that's completely normal. Here are answers to some of the questions I hear most often before a discovery call.

📞 What happens during the Discovery Call?

Our first conversation is relaxed and pressure-free. We'll discuss your goals, your current challenges, and determine whether coaching is the right fit for you.

🔒 Is coaching confidential?

Yes. Your privacy is respected, and our conversations remain confidential except where disclosure is required by law.

🌱 Who do you work with?

LifeStorm Management supports individuals seeking greater clarity, confidence, direction, and personal growth while navigating life's transitions and challenges. While I have a special passion for helping young adults build a strong foundation for their future, I welcome adults of all ages who are ready to prepare, weather, and emerge stronger through life's storms.

📅 How many sessions do clients usually attend?

Every person's journey is unique. Some clients benefit from a few focused sessions, while others choose ongoing coaching for continued growth and accountability.

🧭 Do I need to have everything figured out before we meet?

Not at all. Many clients come to coaching because they're feeling uncertain, overwhelmed, or stuck. Our first conversation is simply a chance to understand where you are and explore what moving forward could look like.
